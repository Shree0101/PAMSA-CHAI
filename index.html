Our Story

Every Big Dream Starts Small.

PAMSA CHAI was born from a simple thought — that a great cup of chai has the power to slow down time, bring people together and create memories.

Five people. Different personalities. Different ideas. One common dream — to build something of our own and share the warmth of chai with the world.

We are not just building a tea brand. We are building a place where conversations begin, friendships grow and every cup tells a story.

Quality & Ingredients

We believe that a great cup begins with great ingredients. We carefully select quality tea leaves, aromatic spices and authentic ingredients to create a flavour that feels familiar yet unforgettable.


No shortcuts. No unnecessary complexity. Just honest ingredients, carefully prepared and served with love.
