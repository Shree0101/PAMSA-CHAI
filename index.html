Why PAMSA CHAI?

Some brands are created in boardrooms. PAMSA CHAI was created around conversations, dreams and a simple love for chai.

PAMSA represents five people coming together with one shared dream — to create a tea brand that feels personal, warm and unforgettable.

We believe a great cup of chai is never just a drink. It is a pause in a busy day. A conversation between friends. A memory shared with family.

Our Belief

A great cup of chai does not just refresh you. It brings people closer.
