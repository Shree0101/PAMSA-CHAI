Our Story

Every big dream starts with a simple cup of chai.

PAMSA CHAI was born from a simple thought — that a cup of chai can bring people together.

Behind PAMSA are five people, one family and one shared dream. A dream to create something of our own, something warm, honest and memorable.

For us, chai is not just a beverage. It is the beginning of conversations, the pause between busy moments and the warmth that brings people closer.

Our journey is just beginning. Every cup we serve is one more step towards building a brand that people remember not only for its taste, but for the feeling it creates.
