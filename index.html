OUR STORY

Every Big Dream Starts Small.

Why PAMSA?

Five people. One family. One dream.

PAMSA CHAI was born from a simple thought — that chai is never just a beverage.

It is a pause in a busy day. A conversation between friends. A memory shared with family. A small moment that feels special.

The name PAMSA represents the five people and five energies behind this dream. Different personalities. One shared vision.

We are starting small, but dreaming big — one cup, one customer and one meaningful conversation at a time.

OUR PROMISE

Quality You Can Taste.

We believe great chai begins with honest ingredients. We focus on flavour, freshness and consistency in every cup. No unnecessary shortcuts. Just good ingredients, thoughtful preparation and a lot of love.

OUR JOURNEY

The Journey Has Just Begun.

Every big dream starts with a small idea. PAMSA CHAI is our family dream — built through ideas, discussions, doubts, planning and the courage to begin.

We may be at the beginning of our journey, but our vision is much bigger. We want to build a tea brand people remember not only for its taste, but also for the warmth they feel when they visit us.
